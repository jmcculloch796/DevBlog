ScotSoft 2018

Date Posted: 28th October 2018

Thanks to e-Placement, Time2Dine got invited to attend the ScotSoft conference at the EICC in Edinburgh. The conference was filled with very interesting talks and I learned a lot from each of them I attended. There was such a variety of topics, so there was something for everyone. I attended talks ranging from technology in the health sector, to penetration testing, to the importance of Open Data and the rise of OpenStreetMaps. We were also invited to attend the Awards Dinner, in which we'd be presented the e-Placement of the year award (which Time2Dine got awarded silver). The night was fantastic, and was topped off with a wonderful after dinner speech from the 2-time Gold Medallist Lizzy Yarnold.
