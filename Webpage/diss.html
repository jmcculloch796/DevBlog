Dissertation

For my 4th year dissertation I have decided to investigate the effectiveness of personal safety applications on smartphones. With the rising popularity of smartphones, and the consistently present threat of crime, there is a gap in the market for personal safety applications. Basically everyone has a phone nowadays, and with this comes the ability to contact anyone at any time, instantly record video and audio, and location tracking. All of these have the ability to reassure people that they are safer, feel more in control.
First I will look into what kind of research has already been done in terms of this, and then gather all of this and apply it to my own study. I then plan to design an app that will take the best features from already existing apps, such as location tracker or panic buttons, but also some of my own original ideas. These will hopefully include an anonymous proximity-based forum, much like yik yak, among others.
I will be updating this page with app designs and thoughts, as well as my blog on the homepage. Wish me luck!
