About Me

Hi, my name is Jamie McCulloch, and I am a fourth year student at Heriot Watt University. I am currently studying a Masters in Software Engineering with the intention of graduating in 2019. Ever since I was a child, my dad would show me how to fix computers, set up printers, and fix the router (by which he meant turn it off and on again). However in the classic way - the student became the teacher - and now he comes to me for help! I grew up knowing I wanted to do something with computers, and when I was in high school and wrote my first Visual Basic "Hello World" program in 4th year high school, I knew I wanted to code.

Now in my fourth year I have worked on a lot of projects within university (see here: projects) ranging from a currency converter Android application to using an A* search algorithm for robots to find their goals. While university has most definitely been challenging, I still have the same passion for computing and technology as I did when I changed the ink in a printer for the first time.

I currently work part time as a bartender in a local bowling alley/golf course, and have done for the last year and a half. However with my time at university diminishing I am currently looking for summer internships for 2018. Also as part of my MEng course, I must undertake a placement for semester 1 of year 5 (September 2018) and do a project and write up based on this.
